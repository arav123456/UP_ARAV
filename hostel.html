This Institute was established in the year 2000 by Manas Seva Samiti, a society formed under the guidance and inspiration of Dr. D.S. Mahalwar, an educationist of repute, primarily as an international Social Center to provide education to the students of Aligarh and neighboring areas of U.P. as well as other states.

The Institute of Information & Management Technology (IIMT Aligarh) made a modest beginning in the year 2000,with a few management & IT programmes and now is one of the best Colleges in Aligarh. Today it has carved a niche for itself notonly in the domain of management education but also in the field of job oriented PG & UG programmes in Computer Science. Teachers Training. Basic Sciences and Paramedical sciences including microbiology and bio technology.

Institute of Information Management and Technology is situated in Aligarh in Uttar Pradesh state of India. Established in 2000, IIMT is a Private college. The college is accredited by AICTE. Institute of Information Management and Technology offers 20 courses across 6 streams namely Management, Education, Science, IT, Commerce and Banking. Popular degrees offered at Institute of Information Management and Technology include BSc, B.Tech, B.Com, BCA, B.Ed. Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, Institute of Information Management and Technology is also a leader in research and innovation. Focus is given to activities beyond academics at Institute of Information Management and Technology, which is evident from its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations. The placement at Institute of Information Management and Technology is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship.
